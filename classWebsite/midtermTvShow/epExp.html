Drifters: Season 1 Episodes

The first six episodes of the Drifters series!

1. Fight Song

In the aftermath of the Battle of Sekigahara, warrior Shimazu Toyohisa is suddenly thrust into a mysterious world where he is shocked to encounter the supposedly Oda Nobunaga and the historical figure Nasu Suketaka Yoichi.

2. Footsteps

As Orte sets the Elven village ablaze in the distance, Nobunaga and Yoichi are dragged into action by Toyohisa's fearless forward charge. Meanwhile on the other side of the world, the march of the Black King is about to Commence.

3. Sortie at Dawn

The Black King’s army marches on the Northern Wall of Carneades while Octobrists try to convince Carneades' defenders to hand over control of the defensive force. This will be the "Ends” debut on the battlefield.

4. Active Heart

The Black King is dealing with the aftermath of the conquest of Carneades. Simultaneously, on the other side of the empire, the Drifters are preparing for backlash and starting to grow their own ambitions.

5. Bring Back Love

As the lord's suppression force marches to the “abandoned” Elven village, a trap laid by the Drifters waits to be sprung. As a result of the clash, the lack of Elven women in the village is explained, and the flames of vengeance ignited.

6. Men of Destiny

As the Elves tear apart the magistrate’s castle, a new player with unknown allegiances looms. Oda starts work on creating the final nail in Orte’s coffin and bringing about a change that will shatter the foundations of the world.
